The Founder

Grounded in Expertise
Shaped by Experience.

Dr. Meghana is an Image Consultant and Personal Colour Analyst with a background in medicine and clinical cosmetology. She holds an advanced certification in Personal Colour Analysis from South Korea , the global benchmark in contemporary colour systems.
In her early years of clinical practice, she recognised that transformation isn’t just about how you look, but how you feel about yourself. Having navigated her own relationship with image and self perception, she understood that confidence isn’t created on the surface; it is built internally and reflected outward.
This led to the creation of The Image Project. Here, she works with individuals who want to take a more thoughtful and strategic approach to how they present themselves.

Her belief is simple: "You already have the power to shape how the world sees you. The Image Project helps you use it with clarity and intention."
